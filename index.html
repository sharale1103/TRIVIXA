About Trivixa

Trivixa ERP is a smart, modular ERP system built for schools, coaching classes, and educational institutions. It simplifies operations across students, staff, inventory, reports, and more. Designed for both metro and rural areas with offline support.

Features

Admin Dashboard

Control staff, students, fees, inventory, and reports from a powerful web interface.

Teacher App

Mark attendance, upload homework, and manage exams on mobile.

Student App

Students can view marks, assignments, notifications, and communicate easily.

Offline Mode

Works in rural or low-connectivity areas using sync technology.

Inventory & Billing

Integrated billing module suitable for jewelry institutes or shop-linked schools.

Custom Modules

Hostel, Transport, Library, and Custom Reports for various use cases.
